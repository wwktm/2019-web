The Conference

The Nepali tech industry is mature with experienced developers, thriving tech-community and promising startups, and this organically developed industry has many inspiring stories to share with the world. Web Weekend aims to connect web technologists throughout the world.

The glocal Nepali tech industry has supported a spectrum of business, ranging from local Nepali agri industry to global Hollywood movies industry. This conference will bring together national and international experts and create an environment for potential partnerships, collaborations and learning groups.

We are organising these events for a bigger cause - connecting Nepal with the global community of Developers, Designers and enthusiasts alike by encouraging professional growth opportunities. Such events support in the skills evolution and nurturing passion for the craft.

Are you with us? We are looking forward to innovative ways of partnerships and are open to make the event fruitful to everyone participating. Have an idea? Talk to us.
